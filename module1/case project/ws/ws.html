Web Security Education

The internet has become an integral part of our lives, but it's important to remember that there are potential risks associated with it. It's crucial to take measures to ensure our safety online. Here are some tips and information on web security:

What Can a Computer Access?

When you visit a website, your browser sends various pieces of information to the server. This information can include:

  • (navigator.app) Your browser's name:
  • (navigator.appVersion)Your browser's version:
  • (navigator.geolocation)Your geolocation:
  • (screen.availHeight)Your screen's height:
  • (screen.availWidth)Your screen's width:
  • (screen.colorDepth)Your screen's color depth:

All this was gatherd from your computer how cool is that. For fun if you changed you computers resolution and refresh the page it will also change on your screen width and height

Tips for Web Security

Here are some tips for staying safe online:

  • Use strong passwords and update them regularly.
  • Never share your passwords with anyone.
  • Use two-factor authentication when available.
  • Keep your software and browsers up-to-date to prevent vulnerabilities.
  • Be cautious of phishing scams and never click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
  • Use a reputable antivirus software and keep it updated.
  • Be careful when sharing personal information online.
  • Use HTTPS whenever possible to ensure secure communication.
